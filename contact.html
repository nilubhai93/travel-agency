Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my trip?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for international trips and 2-3 months for domestic travel. This ensures the best availability and pricing.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we strongly recommend travel insurance for all our packages. We work with trusted providers to offer comprehensive coverage options.

Can I customize a travel package?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom itineraries tailored to your preferences, budget, and travel style.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary by package and provider. We'll provide detailed cancellation terms before you confirm your booking.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer special rates for groups of 6 or more people. Contact us for customized group package pricing.

What documents do I need for international travel?

Requirements vary by destination, but typically include a valid passport and possibly visas. We'll provide specific documentation requirements for your chosen destination.
